Math Decoder

a cryptographic mathematical game for children

Instructions: This application allows parents to generate printable worksheets with a custom secret message which is then encrypted into elementary mathematical expressions based on grade level. In order to decrypt the encrypted message, the child secret agent decoder must solve mathematical expressions to unlock the corresponding letter. Secret agent decoders then can assemble the message one letter at a time to complete the decryption process. Solve all of the math problems and be a cryptographic hero!







This webpage was made with as a creative and engaging solution to promote mathematics in early childhood education. Initially created as a personal project for my own daughter, this web app is free to use and is open source. Feel free to email Eric at eric@ericmcd.com with any questions, comments, or suggestions.
