Math Decoder

a cryptographic mathematical game for children

Instructions: This application allows adults to generate printable worksheets for children with a custom secret message which is then encrypted into elementary mathematical expressions based on grade level. In order to decrypt the encrypted message, the child secret agent decoder must solve mathematical expressions to unlock the corresponding letter. Once letters are associated with solutions, Secret agent decoders then can reassemble the message one letter at a time to complete the decryption process. Solve all of the math problems and be a cryptographic hero!
